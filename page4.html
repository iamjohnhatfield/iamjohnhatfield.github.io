What is Progressive House Music?

Progressive House music is a style of music that emerged in the 1990's in United Kingdom. Party culture in the UK was a lot about house parties and this music was developed to be upbeat and positive. The tempo of the music is fast paced and was meant to be an alternative music style to the classical rock music of the time.
