What is Indie Electronic Music?

Indie Electronic music is rock music with the incorporation of electronic sounds. This genre of music was the next step in rock music for those who wanted to incorprate the new and popular sounds that had begun to gain popularity during the 1990's. One of the most popular bands of this genre is Daft Punk. Alot of the inspiration for the sounds comes from the new synthesizers, samplers, and drum machines that put a new spin on rock and roll music. Check out Indie Electronic music here
