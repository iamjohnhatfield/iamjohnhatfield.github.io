What is Drum & Bass?

Drum & Bass is an electronic music genre that originated from the raven scene in England. This music was popularized in the 1990's like most electronic music at the time. This genre differentiates itself from the others due to its fast breakbeat tempo. This music is generally between 150 and 180 beats per a minute. The music is usually heavy on the bass and is generally only good to listen ot with a good sound system and speakers. headphones without a astrong bass won't allow you to listen to the music the way it's meant to be listened to.
