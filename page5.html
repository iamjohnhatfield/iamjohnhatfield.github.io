What is Chillstep?

Chillstep music is a subgenre of dubstep that was created to give more subtle tones to the fast and impactful beats of dubstep. This subtle vibe that chillstep offers gives a lower BPM at 140 compared to dubstep. Chillstep is more of a relaxing muiscal experience and is not meant ot be party or rave music. This genre was created for those who enjoy dubstep music but want a more subtle sound. Check out Chillstep music here
