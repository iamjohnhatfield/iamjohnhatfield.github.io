What is Electronic Music?

Electronic music is a genre on music that utilizes electronic musical instruments. This genre of music is usually made using certain kinds of devices such as synthesizers and computers. Electronic music has many genres and sub genres that create a different sound. This webiste takes a look at the differences amongst electronic music and the genres that were created from existing music styles.

When Did Electronic Music Gain Popularity in Western Culture?

Electronic music began to gain traction in the 1960's but became widely available to most people during the 1970's. The 1970's is when synthesizers began to be used in a lot of songs which boosted the attraction to this new genre of music.
